# Customer Segment Profiles

## 1. Budget-Conscious Newbies
- **Size**: 125 customers (25%)
- **Average Tenure**: 13.9 months
- **Average Monthly Charges**: $87.50
- **Churn Rate**: 28.0% (Highest)
- **Key Characteristics**: These are relatively new customers with moderate spending. They have a significantly higher churn risk, suggesting they might be testing the service or are sensitive to initial pricing. High concentration of senior citizens (76.8%).

## 2. High-Value Loyalists
- **Size**: 222 customers (44%)
- **Average Tenure**: 41.0 months
- **Average Monthly Charges**: $126.65
- **Churn Rate**: 8.1%
- **Key Characteristics**: Long-term customers with high monthly charges. They show strong loyalty but still have a small churn risk. These are non-senior citizens who value the service quality.

## 3. Premium Spenders
- **Size**: 153 customers (31%)
- **Average Tenure**: 48.5 months
- **Average Monthly Charges**: $116.10
- **Churn Rate**: 0.0%
- **Key Characteristics**: Highly loyal, long-tenured customers. Interestingly, this segment consists entirely of senior citizens in this dataset who are extremely stable and have the lowest price sensitivity relative to tenure.
